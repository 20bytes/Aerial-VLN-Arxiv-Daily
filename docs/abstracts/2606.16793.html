Decay estimates for beam equations with potentials in dimension two

Abstract (EN)

This paper establishes time decay estimates for the following two-dimensional beam (plate) equation with a decaying real-valued potential $V$: \begin{equation*} \partial_t^2 u + (Δ^2 + V) u = 0, \qquad u(0,x)=f(x),\quad \partial_t u(0,x)=g(x). \end{equation*} When zero is a regular point or a first-kind resonance of $H=Δ^2+V$, we first prove sharp $L^1\to L^\infty$ estimates for the solution operators: \begin{align*} \left\|\cos(t\sqrt{H})P_{\mathrm{ac}}(H)\right\|_{L^1\to L^\infty} + \left\|\frac{\sin(t\sqrt{H})}{t\sqrt{H}}P_{\mathrm{ac}}(H)\right\|_{L^1\to L^\infty} \lesssim \frac{1}{|t|}, \end{align*} and obtain an enhanced decay $(|t|\log|t|)^{-1}$ in logarithmically weighted spaces $L^1_ω\to L^\infty_{-ω}$ with $ω(x)=\log(2+|x|)$. For second-kind resonances of $H$ (the bi-Laplacian $Δ^2$ belongs to this class), a non-zero trace moment $\langle |x|^2V,φ\rangle\neq0$ for some second-kind resonance function $φ$ induces severe threshold singularities, worsening the $L^1\to L^\infty$ estimate to $|t|^{-1}(\log|t|)^2$. Finally, for third-kind resonances or a zero eigenvalue, we prove that the presence of $d$-wave resonance leads to the worst $L^1\to L^\infty$ decay rate $\sim(\log|t|)^{-1}$. Several improved estimates are also obtained without a $d$-wave resonance. In particular, in the pure eigenvalue case (i.e., neither $d$-wave nor $p$-wave resonance), both propagators recover the optimal unweighted $L^1\to L^\infty$ estimate $|t|^{-1}.$

摘要 (ZH)

本文建立了以下带有衰减实值势阱$V$的二维梁（板）方程的时间衰减估计： \begin{equation*} \partial_t^2 u + (Δ^2 + V) u = 0, \qquad u(0,x)=f(x),\quad \partial_t u(0,x)=g(x). \end{equation*} 当$H=Δ^2+V$的零点为正则点或第一类共振点时，我们首先证明了解算子的尖锐$L^1\to L^\infty$估计： \begin{align*} \left\|\cos(t\sqrt{H})P_{\mathrm{ac}}(H)\right\|_{L^1\to L^\infty} + \left\|\frac{\sin(t\sqrt{H})}{t\sqrt{H}}P_{\mathrm{ac}}(H)\right\|_{L^1\to L^\infty} \lesssim \frac{1}{|t|}, \end{align*} 并在对数加权空间$L^1_\omega\to L^\infty_{-\omega}$（其中$\omega(x)=\log(2+|x|)$）中获得增强的衰减率$(|t|\log|t|)^{-1}$。 对于$H$的第二类共振点（双拉普拉斯算子$\Delta^2$属于此类），若某第二类共振函数$\phi$满足非零迹矩$\langle |x|^2V,\phi\rangle\neq0$，则会导致严重的阈值奇异性，将$L^1\to L^\infty$估计恶化至$|t|^{-1}(\log|t|)^2$。 最后，对于第三类共振点或零特征值情况，我们证明$d$波共振的存在会导致最差的$L^1\to L^\infty$衰减率$\sim(\log|t|)^{-1}$。在无$d$波共振时，还获得了若干改进估计。特别地，在纯特征值情形（即既无$d$波也无$p$波共振）下，两个传播子均恢复了最优无权重$L^1\to L^\infty$估计$|t|^{-1}$。
